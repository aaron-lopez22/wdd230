Target Audience

The target audience is going to be for people wanting to be healthy and also people who want fruity sweet flavors

For healthy options there will be a display of nutrition for people. With different macros and a list of healthy options.

These people will be fitness enthusiasts or people who want fruit drinks but also want to watch their weight.

The other side of people are the ones who don't care about nutrition but want to have a good drink and don't care about sugar

Sugar drinks will have fun names and be on display and so people who aren't looking for nutrition will want these options
